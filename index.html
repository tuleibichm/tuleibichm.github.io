OUR HISTORY AND MISSION


Adam and Eva matchmaking agency was founded in 2000, those years we operated as traditional dating services agency: Western men and Ukrainian women for marriage corresponded with each other, men could send ladies gifts, and the most important - a man could visit the lady in her City, To communicate with the lady face-to-face and see if the chemistry is there. From the very start we gained reputation of honest and reliable, totally scam-free marriage agency in Ukraine. Since 2006 when IMBRA was implemented, we are fully IMBRA compliant. From the very beginning our mission is to let singles meet and build long-term relationships in the modern world. Real-life meetings between customers are the base of our business. The most popular service we offer is Individual matchmaking tour to Ukraine, this service is very smartly composed and men do not think of lodging, airport transfers etc., and only concentrate on communication with the lady he is coming to visit.

As the time goes, the approach in our business operation changed. We are happy to see how many happy couples we made, and the same time we clearly understood that we could do much better, and make more couples. We noticed very clearly that our gentlemen and ladies both lack the assistance of a dating professional, who could train them, explain them the difference between cultures, explain what is needed to make sure the couple will last. We saw that customers need to be guided, otherwise many of them never be able to find a spouse due to unrealistic expectations, non-settled priorities, etc. By 2009 the owner of the agency was already a trained family psychologist and certified international matchmaker. We implemented new approach in work with our clients, personal matchmaking services. By that time, and now, this service is not very well known in comparison to traditional dating services, mainly because it is highly professional, rare and unique bespoke service in dating. But in some countries, like USA or Australia, or United Kingdom, matchmaking services are very popular.
